Léonore Conte and Caroll Maréchal, accompanied by Thibéry Maillard, went to film the raw footage of an interview with Christophe Jacquet, known as Toffe, and Philippe Gerbaud. In this filmed interview, they present various works created on the Mac between 1983 and 1986.
